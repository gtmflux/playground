🎯 Summary: The Common Thread

The typical car wash prospect in Netchex's pipeline is a small, owner-operated, single-location business with 10–25 employees, located in a Sun Belt or Southern state (primarily TX, FL, TN, AL, NC). The primary decision-maker is the owner or CEO, making this a short buying committee.

The vertical is concentrated — 3 dedicated reps (Duncan, Fonseca, Graham) own 95% of all accounts, suggesting Netchex treats this as a named vertical with specialist coverage.

The 30 companies with 251+ employees are outliers that likely represent multi-site chains and may warrant a separate enterprise track with different messaging and deal structure.
